Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my own videos?
Open the Settings window from the menu bar icon, go to the "Library" tab, and click the "+" button to select video files from your Mac. You can also drag and drop videos directly into the window.
Does it drain my battery?
LiveWallpaper is designed to be energy efficient. It automatically pauses playback when you disconnect from power (if Low Power Mode is enabled) or when you have a fullscreen application open to save resources.
Which video formats are supported?
We support all major video formats compatible with macOS, including MP4, MOV, M4V, and MKV.
Can I use different wallpapers for different screens?
Yes! LiveWallpaper automatically detects all connected displays. You can set a global wallpaper or configure specific playlists for each screen.
