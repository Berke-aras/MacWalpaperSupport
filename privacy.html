Privacy Policy

MacWalpaper

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. MacWalpaper is designed to respect your privacy and data security. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

  • We do not track your usage of the application.
  • We do not collect your IP address or location.
  • We do not have access to your video files or any other files on your computer, other than what you explicitly select within the app.

Local Storage

MacWalpaper stores your preferences (such as volume settings, playlist configurations, and selected videos) locally on your device using standard macOS storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and Application Support directory).

This data never leaves your device.
